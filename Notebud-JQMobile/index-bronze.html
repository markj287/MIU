    Help Section

    Creating Notes

    To create a new note, simple press create new note.
    Next enter a title and a few notes, all other fields are optional.
    All other fields are option. When finished press save!

    Creating Notebooks

    To create a new notebook, simple press create new notebook. You must enter a title. Notebooks are useful to help categorize you various note. Ex: you might want your english class notes separate from your notes on fitness.

    Drawing Notes

    Drawing notes is simple. Just press the draw button and begin drawing.
