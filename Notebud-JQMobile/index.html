Creating Notes

To create a new note, simple press create new note.
Next enter a title and a few notes, all other fields are optional.
All other fields are option. When finished press save!

Creating Notebooks

To create a new notebook, simple press create new notebook. You must enter a title. Notebooks are useful to help categorize you various note. Ex: you might want your english class notes separate from your notes on fitness.

Drawing Notes

Drawing notes is simple. Just press the draw button and begin drawing.

Favorite Movie

My favorite movie

By Mark J on 9/13/2012
I have a lot of favorite movies that i have watched over my years of being on this earth. But my favorite movie of all time is Serendipity staring John Cusack and Kate Beckinsale. Set in the christmas time in New York two complete strangers meet three times in one night, John Cusack and Kate Beckinsale. After the third third time after meeting each other they decided to get to know each other by going on a little date. On that date they felt a connection with each other they never felt before. It was like falling in love on first sight. Fast forward a few months later, Cusack furiously tries to find her right before he gets married to make sure he's not making a mistake by marrying the wrong women. After being unsuccessful at finding her he gives up. Meanwhile Beckinsale tries to find him right before she gets married as well, she gives up after being unsuccessful. In the end they both end up canceling their weddings in hopes of finding each other one more time hoping to capture that magic they had when they first met. Finally in the end they met again at the very spot where they had their first date. This movie is simple heartwarming, it made me cry.
